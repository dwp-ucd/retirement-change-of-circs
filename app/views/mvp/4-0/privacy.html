How we will use your information (PC)

We will treat your information carefully. We may use it for any of our purposes. To learn about your information rights and how we use information, see our personal information charter (opens in new tab). (From Intranet)

As well as the details you provide in your application, we will also use information we already hold about you (and your partner, if you live with one). (PC)

We will use information:

  • that we already have about your State Pension and any benefits you receive
  • that we get from HMRC about your income and earnings

To find out more about how we use information and your information rights, read our Personal Information Charter (opens in new tab).
