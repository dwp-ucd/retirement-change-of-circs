Payment frequency confirmation of change scenarios

The content given to users at confirmation will depend on how close they are to their next payment, whether the payment due will be their first SP payment, and whether they will require a part payment to get onto the right schedule. All scenarios linked from the grey footer of each confirmation page.

  • Scenario 1 - User has a payment due in the next 7 days which is their first payment
  • Scenario 2 - User has a payment due in the next 7 days which is NOT their first payment
  • Scenario 3 - User does not have a payment due in the next 7 days and the next payment is their first payment
  • Scenario 4 - User does not have a payment due in the next 7 days and the next payment is NOT their first payment
  • Scenario 5 - User not have a payment due in the next 7 days, the next payment is their first payment, and the new frequency can be applied from the first payment date without needing a part payment
  • Scenario 6 - User does not have a payment due in the next 7 days, the next payment is NOT their first payment, and the new frequency can be applied from the next cycle without needing a part payment

Alternative journey - only 1 frequency option available

Users must meet specific criteria to be able to change to 2 weekly payments. For those who do not meet the criteria, they will currently only be given 1 option to change to.

  • Change frequency page for users who only have one option available
  • User says no to their one option available
