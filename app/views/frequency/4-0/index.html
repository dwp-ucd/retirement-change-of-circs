Version notes

  • Based on feedback from third round of UR - face-to-face on Thursday 29 June 2023
  • Changes to be tested in a fourth round of testing, with users who have been claiming SP for less than 12 months
  • JIRA ticket for iterations
  • Updated privacy notice page to link to web page version of NI privacy statement for improved accessibility
  • Updated content about NI privacy statement so it's no lgner referred to as 'ours' (DWP's) as it belongs to DfC
  • Referenced that user will need to telephone within 'Get help' content, as i still wasn't clear what form help would take
  • Made 'Get help' contact page into it's own GOV.UK style page, rather than part of 'Contact Pension Service' guide, as this info will not be part of the guides latest update
  • Reverted confirmation page to bulleted layout (from new deferral letters) as the sentence version caused confusion in last round of testing
  • Added content to help users understand why their next 4 weekly payment still had to go through - everyone in testing was confused as to why they couldn't just go straight onto their new chosen frequency payments
  • Updated overview page - all users in testing noticed the frequency had been updated, but commented that it looks like they will be getting £815 every 2 weeks now, even though that is the 4-weekly amount - experimented with content and future payment layouts to find something simple to test next round.

Payment frequency confirmation of change scenarios

The content given to users at confirmation will depend on how close they are to their next payment, whether the payment due will be their first SP payment, and whether they will require a part payment to get onto the right schedule. All scenarios linked from the grey footer of each confirmation page.

  • Scenario 1 - User has first/next payment due in the next 7 days and requires a part payment
  • Scenario 2 - User does not have first/next payment due in the next 7 days and requires a part payment
  • Scenario 3 - New frequency can be applied from the first/next payment date without needing a part payment

Alternative journey - only 1 different frequency option available

Users must meet specific criteria to be able to change to 2 weekly payments. For those who do not meet the criteria, they will currently only be given 1 option to change to.

  • Change frequency page for users who only have one option available
  • User selects to stay on current frequency
